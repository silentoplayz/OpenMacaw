**The Native, Unadulterated AI Agent for macOS.**

OpenMacaw is a world-changing open-source mission to bring a truly native, performant, and privacy-first AI workflow automation agent to Apple Silicon. We are building the "digital ghost in the machine"—reimagining the agentic workflow movement for the M-series era.

## ⚠️ The Problem: The "Dirty" Way
The current landscape of AI desktop agents is fundamentally flawed. They rely on heavy Docker containers that demand complex orchestration, or they are bundled in bloated Electron wrappers that chew through RAM and decimate battery life. They feel foreign, sluggish, and detached from the underlying operating system. 

For Mac professionals who demand premium, native experiences, these solutions are undeniably **"dirty."** We refuse to accept this compromise.

## ⚡️ The Macaw Way: Native Apple Silicon
OpenMacaw represents a paradigm shift. We are harnessing the unbridled power of Apple hardware, utilizing the **MLX framework** and **Metal** to run local intelligence at blazing speeds directly on the Unified Memory Architecture.

- **Zero Overhead:** OpenMacaw runs as a lightweight, lightning-fast macOS daemon.
- **Privacy as a Default:** Inference happens in the Secure Enclave. No cloud dependencies. No telemetry. Your data never leaves your Mac.
- **SwiftUI Native:** A pure interface designed to feel like a part of the OS, not a website in a box.

## 🦜 The 'Parrot' Engine
At the core of OpenMacaw lies the **Parrot Engine**. Rather than relying on fragile web-DOM scraping or generic cloud-based vision, the Parrot Engine hooks directly into native **macOS Accessibility APIs (AXUIElement)** and **ScreenCaptureKit**.

It observes your workflows, learns your patterns, and seamlessly replicates them. It doesn't just click coordinates; it natively *understands* the application's UI hierarchy, allowing it to "parrot" your actions across any desktop application with near-zero latency.
